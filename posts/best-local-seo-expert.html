What Is Local SEO? A Complete Explanation

Local SEO (Local Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing your online presence so your business appears prominently when people in your area search for the products or services you offer. It is a specialized branch of SEO that focuses entirely on location-based search visibility.

Unlike traditional SEO that targets national or global audiences, local SEO zeroes in on the customers physically near your business or in your target service area. It targets searches that contain location qualifiers like:

  • "best digital marketing agency in Dhaka"
  • "SEO expert near me"
  • "top restaurant Gulshan Bangladesh"
  • "plumber in Uttara"

In 2026, 46% of all Google searches have local intent — meaning nearly half of everyone searching on Google is looking for something near them. If your business is not optimized for local search, you are invisible to almost half of your potential customers.

The Google Local 3-Pack — The Most Valuable Real Estate on Google

The Local 3-Pack is the block of three business listings that appears at the very top of Google results for local searches, above even the organic results. It includes a map, business name, rating, address, hours, and phone number. Studies show the Local 3-Pack captures 44% of all local search clicks. Getting your business into the Local 3-Pack is the single highest-ROI goal in local SEO — and it is exactly what I specialize in as your best local SEO expert.

Local SEO vs. National SEO — What Is the Difference?

Many business owners confuse local SEO with general SEO. Understanding the difference is critical for choosing the right strategy for your business.

Local SEO

  • Targets customers in a specific city, area, or region
  • Focuses on Google Maps, Local 3-Pack, and "near me" searches
  • Centers on Google Business Profile, citations, and local reviews
  • Produces results faster — typically 4 to 16 weeks
  • Ideal for restaurants, clinics, salons, law firms, contractors, retail stores

National SEO

  • Targets customers across an entire country or globally
  • Focuses on organic rankings in standard Google search results
  • Centers on content, authority backlinks, and technical SEO
  • Results typically take 3 to 12 months in competitive niches
  • Ideal for e-commerce, SaaS, media, and national service brands
